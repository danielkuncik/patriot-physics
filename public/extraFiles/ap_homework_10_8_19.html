3. A short self-done lab:

Get a really heavy box and try to push it across the floor. Ask yourself whether it was harder to start the box moving or keep it moving. To make the box moving, you must overcome maximum static friction, and to keep it moving you must push with a force equal to the force of kinetic friction. Which is stronger: the maximum static friction or the force of kinetic friction?
