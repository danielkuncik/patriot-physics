    • Describe the motion of the cannonball in the horizontal dimension. What equation can be used to quantitatively analyze this motion?
    • Describe the motion of the cannonball in the vertical dimension. What equations can be used to quantitatively analyze this motion?

    • Determine the range that the cannonball travels before striking the ground.
